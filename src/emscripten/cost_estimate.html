Basis for the defaults

  • Rate. North-American freelance illustration in 2025 spans roughly $45/hr (emerging) to $95+/hr (senior / specialised children's & game art). $65 is a mid-professional midpoint — bump it up for a sought-after artist.
  • Backgrounds are the heaviest line (full painted scenes), props are quick, and animation frames are cheap per frame once Gina's design exists — which is why that one-time design cost is separate.
  • Revisions are modelled as extra passes costing a fraction of an asset's base time; two rounds at 25% ≈ +50% on production.
  • Counts default to Gina's current placeholder inventory (assets/index.json): 5 backgrounds, 10 props, 11 animations / 43 frames. Edit them to price a different scope.
  • Excludes voice/audio, in-engine integration (stitching sheets is automated — see the asset pipeline), and any licensing/rush premiums.
